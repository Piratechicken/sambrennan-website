Professor Samantha Brennan explores contemporary morality, with a focus on applied ethics, political and feminist theory. She has also worked on on children's rights, family justice, gender & sexuality, death and fashion.

Brennan is currently a professor in the Department of Women's Studies and Feminist Research at Western University, Canada.
