Brennan has broad ranging research interests in feminist ethics, applied ethics, social and political philosophy, and feminist theory. She's published about children's rights and family justice, normative ethics, rights theories, gender and sexuality, death, and fashion.
Brennan is currently a professor in the Department of Women's Studies and Feminist Research at Western University, Canada. She is also a member of the Rotman Institute of Philosophy and a member of the graduate faculty of the Departments of Philosophy and Political Science.
